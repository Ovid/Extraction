## Important Issues

### [I1] METHODOLOGY.md not updated for v2lgcrrpt and wb_top10_income indicators
- **File:** `METHODOLOGY.md:15-50`
- **Bug:** Two new indicators are active in the scoring pipeline but absent from METHODOLOGY.md. `v2lgcrrpt` (Legislative Corruption) is used in the `political_capture` domain at `score_countries.py:1784`, but the Political Capture indicator table in METHODOLOGY.md only lists 4 V-Dem variables. `wb_top10_income` (Income share of top 10%) is configured at `score_countries.py:75-78` for `economic_concentration`, but the Economic Concentration table lists only Gini and Labour income share.
- **Impact:** Violates CLAUDE.md rule: "Changes to scoring methodology must be reflected in METHODOLOGY.md." Anyone auditing the methodology will see fewer indicators documented than are in production, undermining trust.
- **Suggested fix:** Add rows to the respective indicator tables:
  - Political Capture: `| Legislative Corruption | V-Dem | v2lgcrrpt | Inverted |`
  - Economic Concentration: `| Income share of top 10% | World Bank | SI.DST.10TH.10 | Direct |`
- **Confidence:** High
- **Found by:** Contract & Integration

### [I2] selectedCountryCode not cleared when selecting a no-data country
- **File:** `js/app.js:294-301`
- **Bug:** When `selectCountry()` is called for a country with no data in `scores.json`, it returns early at line 298 without clearing `selectedCountryCode`. The stale value persists, so weight slider handlers (lines 563-570, 585-592) recompute the composite for the *previously selected* country while the map visually highlights the new (no-data) country.
- **Impact:** Visual mismatch between map selection and side panel data. User adjusts sliders expecting to affect the highlighted country but updates are applied to the old selection.
- **Suggested fix:** Set `selectedCountryCode = null` before the early return at line 298.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Found by:** Concurrency & State
